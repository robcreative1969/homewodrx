What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — like whether you're logged in — across page loads and sessions.

Cookies We Use

HomeWodRX uses the following categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function. These handle your login session via Supabase. Without these, you cannot stay logged in.
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics use cookies to collect anonymous data about how visitors use the site (pages visited, time on page, referral source). This helps us improve the site. No personally identifiable information is collected.
We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to ad networks.

Controlling Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from staying logged in to your account.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as the site evolves and new technologies are introduced. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
